Privacy Policy

How Strimo handles your privacy and data

Last updated: June 16, 2026 ・ Version 1.1
Summary

Summary

Strimo's privacy approach in a nutshell

Your health data is used only for the game.
  • HealthKit step, sleep, and workout data is used solely for in-game growth calculations.
  • It is never used for advertising and never sold or shared with third parties.
  • You can delete your account from within the app at any time.
Collect

Information We Collect

The types of information the app handles

🆔

Information you provide

Apple ID (for authentication, required) and display name (for in-game display and rankings, optional). You can also play as a guest without signing in.

👟

HealthKit data

Step, sleep, and workout data. Steps earn Step Points, sleep aids monster recovery, and workouts grant bonuses. Use requires your explicit permission.

📍

Approximate location

Used for pass-by encounters and for weather- and environment-based bonuses and which monsters appear.

🛡️

Automatically collected

A device identifier to prevent abuse, and crash reports (MetricKit) to improve app quality.

HealthKit

How We Handle HealthKit Data

Health data is treated with special care

Our promises about HealthKit data
  • Step, sleep, and workout data is used only to provide the app's game features.
  • Using Apple HealthKit requires your explicit permission.
  • HealthKit data is never used for advertising.
  • HealthKit data is never shared with or sold to third parties.
  • HealthKit data is not backed up to iCloud.

Your Rights

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information

  • Access: the right to access and review your personal information
  • Correction: the right to request correction of your personal information
  • Deletion: the right to request deletion of your personal information

How to delete your account: You can delete it from Settings > Account in the app. Deletion removes or anonymizes your account info, game data, SP balance, and posted content. However, information required for legal compliance, abuse prevention, billing records, etc. may be retained to the minimum extent for a limited period. Deleted data generally cannot be restored.

Children

Children's Privacy

This app is intended for ages 9 and up. Those under 13 should use it with parental consent. Parents may request to review, delete, or stop the collection of their child's personal information.
